Maldives

Maldives is a low lying island country in the Indian Ocean, it is 7 1 7 km from Sri Lanka and 430 km from India. There are 1192 islands which are dispersed over an area of 90000 square kilometers southwest of Sri Lanka. Every island is individual and seperate from other islands, there is a lagoon or sea in between the islands.

A Islands of Maldives are beautiful with white sandy beaches and clear water seas that fade away into deep ocean blue. Almost all islands have at least part of the beach that has fine sand. Resorts are mainly developed in islands that have good quality beach and rich sea.

Atolls

The word atoll is believed to be derived from local Maldivian word "altholhu" which means group of islands. There are 26 natural atolls which are offcicially recognized as 20 for administrative purposes. Not all atolls are of same size or hold same number of islands, it is based on geographical grouping.

Archipelago

Maldives is an archipelago of islands. Archipelago means chain of atolls. The Maldives archipelago is made up of 26 natural atolls streched over 90000 square kilometer in the sea which is filled with hundreds of tiny coral islands, reefs and mariane lives.

More about Maldives' Geography

Reefs

Tiny coral islands of the Maldives are protected by two major types of reefs; house reef and outer reef. There are islands that have lagoon fading into the blue ocean without any barrier in between. Other Islands have house reef where lagoon meets deep sea. The house reef protects lagoon and the beach of the island from waves and ocean current.

Volcanoes

It is believed that the atolls are actually super volcanoes. The circular pattern in formation of atoll is the rim of the volcano. Don't worry, these volcanoes are inactive and that enables colourful colors to grow and florish top.

Earthquakes

Despite millions of years old volcanoes the Maldives is not prone to earthquake. Meteorological Department at times reported some earthquakes in the Maldives region but it was too minor for people to feel anything.
